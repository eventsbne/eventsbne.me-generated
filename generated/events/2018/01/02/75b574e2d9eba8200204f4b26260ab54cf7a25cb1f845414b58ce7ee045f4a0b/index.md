<H2><SPAN>Are you interested in learning to use computers or the internet, but not sure where to start?</SPAN></H2>

<P><SPAN>Topics include:</SPAN></P>
<OL>
<LI><SPAN><SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN>Beginner computing skills</SPAN><SPAN></SPAN></LI>
<LI><SPAN></SPAN><SPAN>Microsoft Word 2016</SPAN></LI>
<LI><SPAN></SPAN><SPAN>Using the internet</SPAN></LI>
<LI><SPAN><SPAN></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN>Introduction to email</SPAN></LI>
</OL>
